Project Overview

T-Mobile needed to simplify their carrier switching process to reduce customer friction and increase successful migrations from other carriers.

The existing switching process was complex, with multiple steps and unclear instructions that led to high abandonment rates. Users often got confused about requirements and next steps.

Project Details

  • Role: Senior UX Designer
  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Team: 4 designers, 3 developers
  • Tools: Figma, Axure, UserTesting
  • Platform: Mobile-first Web

The Problem

User Pain Points

  • Complex, multi-step switching process
  • Unclear requirements and documentation needed
  • Poor mobile experience with tiny form fields
  • No progress indication or clear next steps
  • High abandonment rates during the process
  • Confusing terminology and industry jargon

Business Goals

  • Increase successful carrier switches by 35%
  • Reduce process abandonment by 50%
  • Improve mobile conversion rates
  • Enhance customer satisfaction scores
  • Streamline support ticket volume
  • Create scalable design system for future flows

Approach & Process

Research

Analyzed user behavior data, conducted usability testing, and mapped the existing customer journey to identify pain points and opportunities.

Design

Created mobile-first wireframes and prototypes focusing on progressive disclosure, clear progress indication, and simplified language.

Implementation

Worked closely with development team to ensure responsive design, accessibility compliance, and seamless integration with backend systems.

Outcomes & Impact

Performance Improvements

  • 42% increase in successful switches
  • 58% reduction in process abandonment
  • 35% improvement in mobile conversion
  • 85% user satisfaction score
  • 40% reduction in support tickets

Key Features Delivered

  • Simplified 3-step switching process
  • Mobile-optimized forms with smart validation
  • Clear progress indicator and next steps
  • Plain language explanations and tooltips
  • Accessible design with keyboard navigation
  • Real-time status updates and notifications
