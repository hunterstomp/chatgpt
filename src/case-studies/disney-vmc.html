Lessons Learned

Key insights from designing the Disney VMC virtual experiences

Immersion Requires Intuition

Virtual experiences must feel natural and intuitive to maintain immersion and prevent users from being pulled out of the magical world.

Storytelling Drives Engagement

Compelling narratives and character interactions are essential for maintaining user engagement in virtual environments.

Magic Moments Matter

Unexpected, delightful interactions and surprise elements create memorable experiences that users want to share and return to.

Performance Enables Wonder

Smooth, responsive virtual experiences are crucial for maintaining the sense of magic and preventing technical frustrations.
