F.A.Q.uentify

What's your design process?

I follow a user-centered design approach: Research → Define → Design → Prototype → Test → Iterate. Each project starts with understanding user needs and business goals, then evolves through collaborative ideation and rigorous testing.

What industries do you work in?

I've designed for telecommunications (T-Mobile, AT&T), technology (Microsoft), entertainment (Disney), healthcare, eCommerce, and emerging tech. My experience spans B2B, B2C, and enterprise solutions across multiple platforms.

Do you work remotely?

Yes! I'm experienced with remote collaboration and can work effectively across time zones. I use modern tools for design collaboration, user research, and stakeholder communication to ensure seamless project delivery.

How do I get started?

Simple! Send me an email at design@q10ux.com or call me at (206) 427-9129 . I'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project goals, timeline, and how I can help bring your vision to life.

What tools and technologies do you use?

I'm proficient with Figma, Sketch, Adobe Creative Suite, Axure, and modern prototyping tools. I also work with design systems, component libraries, and collaborate effectively with development teams using tools like Zeplin, InVision, and modern design-to-code workflows.
