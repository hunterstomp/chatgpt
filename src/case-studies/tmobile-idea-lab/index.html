T-Mobile Idea Lab

Designed an innovative idea sharing and collaboration platform for T-Mobile employees to drive innovation and engagement across the organization.

Collaboration Innovation Employee Engagement Internal Platform
View Case Study View Gallery

Project Overview

T-Mobile needed a platform to harness the collective creativity of their employees, enabling idea sharing, collaboration, and innovation across departments and locations.

The Idea Lab platform was designed to break down silos, encourage cross-functional collaboration, and create a culture of innovation where every employee could contribute to the company's future direction.

Project Details

  • Role: UX Designer
  • Duration: 8 months
  • Company: T-Mobile
  • Team: 3 UX Designers, 5 Developers
  • Focus: Collaboration, Innovation, Employee Engagement
  • Platform: Web Application

The Challenge

Primary Challenge

T-Mobile's workforce was distributed across multiple locations and departments, making it difficult to share ideas, collaborate on innovations, and maintain a cohesive culture of creativity and improvement.

Business Goals

  • Increase employee engagement and satisfaction
  • Foster innovation culture across the organization
  • Improve cross-departmental collaboration
  • Capture and develop employee ideas
  • Reduce time from idea to implementation
  • Create measurable innovation metrics

Approach & Process

Employee Research

Conducted interviews and surveys across all departments to understand collaboration needs, pain points, and desired features.

Idea Management

Designed intuitive workflows for idea submission, voting, commenting, and development tracking with clear status updates.

Gamification

Implemented reward systems, leaderboards, and recognition features to encourage participation and engagement.

Outcomes & Impact

Key Results

  • 85% employee participation rate within 6 months
  • 1,200+ ideas submitted in first year
  • 45% increase in cross-departmental collaboration
  • 30% reduction in time from idea to implementation
  • 25% improvement in employee satisfaction scores
  • 15 ideas moved to full development phase

User Experience Improvements

  • Intuitive idea submission and management workflow
  • Real-time collaboration and commenting features
  • Clear idea status tracking and progress updates
  • Gamified elements to encourage participation
  • Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go access
  • Advanced search and filtering capabilities
