Project Overview

Microsoft needed to modernize the Office 365 experience to meet the evolving needs of modern workplaces, emphasizing collaboration, accessibility, and seamless cross-platform functionality.

The redesign focused on creating a unified experience across desktop, web, and mobile platforms while maintaining the familiar Office interface that users know and trust.

Project Details

  • Role: UX Designer
  • Duration: 12 months
  • Company: Microsoft
  • Team: 8 UX Designers, 15 Developers
  • Focus: Productivity, Collaboration, Cloud Computing
  • Platform: Desktop, Web, Mobile

The Challenge

Primary Challenge

Office 365 needed to evolve from a traditional desktop suite to a modern, cloud-based collaboration platform while maintaining backward compatibility and user familiarity across diverse devices and use cases.

Business Goals

  • Improve user adoption and engagement
  • Enhance collaboration capabilities
  • Ensure accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA)
  • Optimize for cloud-first workflows
  • Maintain cross-platform consistency
  • Reduce learning curve for new users

Approach & Process

User Research

Conducted extensive user research across enterprise, education, and consumer segments to understand collaboration patterns and pain points.

Cloud-First Design

Redesigned core workflows to prioritize cloud collaboration, real-time editing, and seamless device switching.

Accessibility Focus

Implemented comprehensive accessibility features including screen reader support, keyboard navigation, and high contrast modes.

Outcomes & Impact

Key Results

  • 40% increase in collaboration features usage
  • 35% improvement in user satisfaction scores
  • WCAG 2.1 AA compliance achieved
  • 50% reduction in support tickets
  • 25% increase in mobile app engagement
  • Improved cross-platform consistency scores

User Experience Improvements

  • Streamlined collaboration workflows
  • Enhanced real-time editing capabilities
  • Improved accessibility across all platforms
  • Better mobile responsiveness and touch interactions
  • Simplified sharing and permission management
  • Consistent design language across applications
