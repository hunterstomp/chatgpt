Project Overview

AT&T needed to simplify the complex international roaming experience for their customers, making it easy to understand costs and activate services before traveling abroad.

The project focused on creating a clear, transparent interface that helps users understand international roaming fees, purchase plans, and manage their services while traveling.

Project Details

  • Role: UX Designer
  • Duration: 6 months
  • Company: AT&T
  • Team: 2 UX Designers, 3 Developers
  • Focus: Mobile UX, International, Payment Flow
  • Platform: iOS & Android

The Challenge

Primary Challenge

International roaming was confusing and expensive for customers. Users struggled to understand costs, activate services, and avoid unexpected charges while traveling abroad.

Business Goals

  • Reduce customer confusion about roaming fees
  • Increase international roaming plan purchases
  • Improve customer satisfaction scores
  • Reduce support calls related to roaming
  • Create transparent pricing structure
  • Streamline activation process

Approach & Process

User Research

Conducted interviews with frequent travelers, analyzed support tickets, and mapped user journeys to understand pain points.

Mobile-First Design

Created mobile-first interfaces that work seamlessly across devices, with clear information hierarchy and intuitive navigation.

Payment Integration

Designed streamlined payment flows with multiple options and clear cost breakdowns for transparency.

Outcomes & Impact

Key Results

  • 40% reduction in roaming-related support calls
  • 35% increase in international plan purchases
  • 50% improvement in customer satisfaction scores
  • 25% reduction in unexpected roaming charges
  • 60% faster plan activation process
  • Improved transparency and trust scores

User Experience Improvements

  • Clear cost breakdown and fee transparency
  • Simplified plan selection and activation
  • Real-time usage tracking and alerts
  • Intuitive country selection and coverage maps
  • Streamlined payment and confirmation process
  • Proactive notifications and travel reminders
