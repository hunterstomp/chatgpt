How to Use Smart Upload
What is Smart Upload?

Smart Upload automatically processes your media files and organizes them for your portfolio. It's like having a personal assistant for your UX work!

How to Upload
  1. Drag & Drop: Simply drag files or folders onto the upload area
  2. Click to Browse: Click "Choose Files" to select files manually
  3. Watch Folder: Drop files in the uploads folder for automatic processing
Automatic Tagging

The system automatically detects what type of UX work your files represent based on their names:

  • user-research-notes.jpg ‚Üí User Research
  • wireframe-homepage.png ‚Üí Wireframes
  • prototype-mobile.fig ‚Üí Prototypes
Cloud Integration

Files are automatically uploaded to your cloud storage (AWS S3 or Sanity) for backup and sharing.
