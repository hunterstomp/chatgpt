AT&T International Roaming

Designing seamless global connectivity experiences for international travelers

Global Connectivity International UX Mobile Design Cross-Cultural Design
Challenge

Create an intuitive international roaming experience that works seamlessly across different countries, cultures, and mobile networks.

Solution

Designed a comprehensive international roaming platform with clear pricing, easy activation, and real-time usage tracking.

Result

Increased international roaming adoption by 45% and reduced customer support calls by 60% through improved user experience.

1

Research

Understanding international travel and connectivity needs

Global Travel Research

Comprehensive research into international travel patterns, connectivity pain points, and user needs across different regions and cultures.

  • International traveler interviews and surveys
  • Analysis of roaming usage patterns and pain points
  • Competitive research of international carriers
  • Cultural considerations for different regions
  • Technical analysis of global network infrastructure

Key Insights

  • Travelers feared unexpected charges and bill shock
  • Activation process was confusing and time-consuming
  • Usage tracking was unclear and unreliable
  • Different countries had varying connectivity needs

Strategy

Defining the international roaming experience strategy

Global Connectivity Strategy

Developed a comprehensive strategy for creating a seamless international roaming experience that addresses user concerns and business objectives.

  • Defined international traveler personas and journey maps
  • Created transparent pricing and billing strategies
  • Established clear activation and usage tracking processes
  • Planned integration with AT&T's global network partners
  • Designed multi-language and cultural adaptation strategies

Strategic Pillars

  • Transparency - Clear pricing and usage information
  • Simplicity - Easy activation and management
  • Reliability - Consistent connectivity across countries
  • Control - User-managed usage and spending limits

Lessons Learned

Key insights from designing the AT&T International Roaming experience

Transparency Builds Trust

Clear pricing and real-time usage tracking are essential for building customer trust in international roaming services.

Cultural Considerations Matter

International products require careful consideration of cultural differences, language preferences, and local user behaviors.

Simplicity Reduces Friction

Complex international processes need to be simplified to reduce user friction and increase adoption rates.

Real-time Feedback is Critical

Providing immediate feedback on usage and costs helps users make informed decisions and avoid bill shock.
