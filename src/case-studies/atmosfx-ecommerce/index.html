Project Overview

AtmosFX needed a complete eCommerce redesign to improve user experience, accessibility, and seasonal storytelling capabilities on their Shopify platform.

The project focused on creating an intuitive shopping experience that celebrates seasonal content while ensuring accessibility compliance and driving conversions through improved user flows and visual design.

Project Details

  • Role: UX Designer
  • Duration: 3 months
  • Company: AtmosFX
  • Team: 1 UX Designer, 1 Developer
  • Focus: eCommerce, Accessibility, Seasonal UX
  • Platform: Shopify

The Challenge

Primary Challenge

The existing eCommerce site had poor accessibility, confusing navigation, and didn't effectively showcase seasonal content. Users struggled to find products and complete purchases.

Business Goals

  • Improve conversion rates by 25%
  • Enhance accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA)
  • Create seasonal storytelling capabilities
  • Streamline checkout process
  • Improve mobile experience
  • Increase average order value

Approach & Process

User Research

Conducted user interviews, analytics review, and accessibility audit to understand pain points and opportunities.

Design & Prototyping

Created wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, and interactive prototypes focusing on accessibility and seasonal storytelling.

Implementation

Collaborated with developer to implement the design on Shopify, ensuring accessibility compliance and performance optimization.

Outcomes & Impact

Key Results

  • 30% increase in conversion rate
  • 45% improvement in mobile engagement
  • WCAG 2.1 AA compliance achieved
  • 25% reduction in cart abandonment
  • 40% increase in seasonal content engagement
  • Improved accessibility scores across all metrics

User Experience Improvements

  • Streamlined product discovery and navigation
  • Enhanced seasonal storytelling capabilities
  • Improved checkout flow and payment options
  • Better mobile responsiveness and performance
  • Accessible design for all users
  • Clear product categorization and filtering
