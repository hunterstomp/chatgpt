Research

Understanding customer shopping behavior and product discovery needs

Customer Shopping Research

Comprehensive research into how customers discover, evaluate, and purchase atmospheric entertainment products online.

  • Customer interviews and shopping behavior analysis
  • Competitive analysis of entertainment e-commerce sites
  • User journey mapping for product discovery
  • Analysis of existing AtmosFX customer feedback
  • Market research on entertainment product trends

Key Insights

  • Customers struggled to find products that matched their specific needs
  • Product descriptions lacked sufficient detail for decision-making
  • Checkout process was complex and had high abandonment rates
  • Mobile experience was not optimized for product browsing

Lessons Learned

Key insights from designing the AtmosFX E-commerce platform

Product Discovery is Key

Making it easy for customers to find the right products is crucial for e-commerce success, especially for specialized entertainment products.

Mobile-First Design Matters

A significant portion of e-commerce traffic comes from mobile devices, making responsive design essential for success.

Simplified Checkout Converts

Reducing friction in the checkout process significantly improves conversion rates and reduces cart abandonment.

Data-Driven Optimization Works

Continuous monitoring and optimization based on user behavior data leads to sustained improvements in performance.
