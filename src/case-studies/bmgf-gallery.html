Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation

Designing a digital platform that connects people with global philanthropic initiatives and impact stories

Philanthropic Design Content Strategy Digital Engagement Impact Storytelling
Challenge

Create a digital platform that effectively communicates the Foundation's mission and impact while engaging diverse global audiences.

Solution

Designed a comprehensive digital experience with compelling storytelling, interactive content, and clear pathways for engagement.

Result

Increased engagement by 120%, improved content discoverability by 85%, and enhanced global reach and impact awareness.

1

Research

Understanding philanthropic engagement and content consumption patterns

Philanthropic Engagement Research

Comprehensive research into how people engage with philanthropic content and what motivates them to learn about and support global initiatives.

  • Audience research across different demographics and regions
  • Analysis of philanthropic content consumption patterns
  • Competitive research of other foundation websites
  • User journey mapping for content discovery
  • Stakeholder interviews with Foundation staff and partners

Key Insights

  • Audiences wanted more personal, human stories behind the data
  • Content was difficult to navigate and discover
  • Mobile experience was not optimized for content consumption
  • Impact stories needed better visual presentation

Developed a comprehensive strategy for creating a digital platform that effectively communicates the Foundation's mission and impact.

Strategic Pillars

  • Storytelling - Compelling narratives that humanize impact
  • Accessibility - Content that reaches diverse global audiences

  • Transparency - Clear communication of impact and progress

Lessons Learned

Key insights from designing the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation digital platform

Storytelling Drives Engagement

Human-centered storytelling is essential for connecting audiences with philanthropic impact and fostering meaningful engagement.

Accessibility Enables Impact

Inclusive design ensures that philanthropic content reaches diverse global audiences and maximizes social impact.

Transparency Builds Trust

Clear communication of impact, progress, and challenges builds trust and credibility with stakeholders and audiences.

Mobile-First Reaches More

Mobile-optimized experiences are crucial for reaching global audiences, especially in regions with limited desktop access.
