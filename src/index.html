My UX Design Process

A proven 8-phase methodology that delivers exceptional user experiences

1
Research

Understanding users, market, and opportunities through comprehensive research methods including user interviews, competitive analysis, and stakeholder interviews.

2
Strategy

Defining direction and business alignment through user personas, journey mapping, feature prioritization, and success metrics definition.

3
Ideation

Exploring concepts and solutions through collaborative ideation, concept generation, and evaluation of multiple solution approaches.

4
Design

Creating detailed interfaces and interactions through wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, and design system development.

5
Prototyping

Building interactive prototypes to test user flows and interactions, including motion design and animation specifications.

6
Testing

Validating with users through usability testing, A/B testing, accessibility testing, and cross-browser compatibility testing.

7
Implementation

Bringing designs to life through close collaboration with engineering teams, ensuring pixel-perfect implementation.

8
Launch

Going live and measuring success through analytics, user feedback, and continuous improvement based on real-world usage.
