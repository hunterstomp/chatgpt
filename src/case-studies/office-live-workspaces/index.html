Office Live Workspaces

Designed a future vision for Microsoft Office Live Workspaces, exploring social collaboration and cloud-based document sharing with animated presentations and interactive prototypes.

Project Overview

Microsoft needed to explore the future of collaborative document creation and sharing, envisioning how Office Live Workspaces could evolve into a social, cloud-based platform that connects people through their work.

This future vision project focused on creating interactive prototypes and animated presentations that demonstrated the potential for social collaboration, real-time editing, and seamless cloud-based workflows.

Project Details

  • Role: UX Designer
  • Duration: 6 months
  • Company: Microsoft
  • Team: 3 UX Designers, 2 Developers
  • Focus: Future Vision, Social Collaboration, Cloud Computing
  • Platform: Web & Interactive Prototypes

The Challenge

Primary Challenge

Microsoft needed to envision how Office Live Workspaces could evolve beyond traditional document sharing to become a social platform that connects people through collaborative work and real-time interaction.

Business Goals

  • Explore future collaboration possibilities
  • Demonstrate social features integration
  • Showcase cloud-based workflows
  • Create compelling vision presentations
  • Validate user interest in social collaboration
  • Guide future product development

Approach & Process

Future Visioning

Explored emerging trends in social collaboration, cloud computing, and user behavior to envision next-generation workspaces.

Social Integration

Designed features that integrate social networking concepts with document collaboration and workspace management.

Interactive Prototypes

Created animated presentations and interactive prototypes to demonstrate the vision and gather stakeholder feedback.

Outcomes & Impact

Key Results

  • Successfully presented vision to Microsoft leadership
  • Generated interest in social collaboration features
  • Influenced future Office 365 development
  • Created compelling animated presentations
  • Validated user interest in social workspaces
  • Established design patterns for future products

User Experience Improvements

  • Social collaboration features and user profiles
  • Real-time activity feeds and notifications
  • Cloud-based document sharing and editing
  • Interactive workspace management
  • Seamless cross-device synchronization
  • Community-driven content discovery
